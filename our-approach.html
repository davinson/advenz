Our mission

Use fast, safe, and reliable wireless power to help people, businesses, and the planet thrive.

When we think of our best days, we think of a world without wires. Where without plugging in, power is transferred so quickly, efficiently, and safely, that it feels like magic. We're leading the movement to wirelessly electrify work, logistics, transportation, and patient care so we can have more of our best days, and so we can keep our economy and our planet healthy for generations to come.
